Featured Artists

Juan Karlos

Juan Karlos has quickly become one of the most influential voices in Filipino music. Since his breakout single "Buwan" in 2018, the singer-songwriter has garnered widespread acclaim, captivating audiences with his unique blend of rock, indie, and pop. His debut album *JK* and his latest releases showcase his exceptional talent, cementing his place as a defining artist of his generation.

Moira Dela Torre

Moira Dela Torre has become one of the most captivating voices in Filipino music. Known for her heartfelt ballads, she gained widespread recognition with her breakout hit "Love Me Instead" and her emotional rendition of "Tagpuan." With multiple chart-topping singles and albums, Moira continues to touch hearts with her raw vulnerability, solidifying her place as one of the most influential artists of her generation.

Tj Monterde

TJ Monterde has quickly established himself as one of the most beloved voices in contemporary Filipino music. Known for his heartfelt and relatable songs, he gained widespread attention with hits like "Ikaw at Ako" and "Pasensya Na." With his unique blend of pop, acoustic, and R&B influences, TJ continues to captivate audiences and solidify his place as a leading artist in the Filipino music scene.
